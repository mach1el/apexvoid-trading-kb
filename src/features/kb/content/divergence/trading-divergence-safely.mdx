# Trading Divergence Safely

Divergence alone is a weak signal. An asset with strong momentum can diverge on an oscillator for an extended period before finally reversing. To trade divergence safely, you must pair it with a shift in market structure, specifically a Change of Character (CHoCH).

## The Divergence + CHoCH Setup

A Change of Character occurs when price breaks the structural point that created the last extreme (high or low). When divergence appears, we wait for a CHoCH to confirm that the trend has actually reversed.

1.  **Identify Divergence:** Spot regular divergence between price and your oscillator.
2.  **Wait for Structure Break:** Wait for price to break the last lower high (for bullish reversals) or higher low (for bearish reversals).
3.  **Enter on Retest:** Once the CHoCH occurs, wait for price to retest a newly formed supply or demand zone.
