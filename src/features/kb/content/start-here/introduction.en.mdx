# Welcome to ApexVoid Trading Knowledge

This knowledge base is a structured, framework-neutral resource for learning how price moves and how traders read that movement. It covers the core ideas behind **market structure**, **key levels**, **price action**, **order blocks**, **SMC**, **ICT concepts**, and **confluence** — without selling any single method as "the answer."

---

## Who This Is For

<ConceptCard title="Target Audience" icon="🎯">
  <p>This resource is designed for traders who want to <strong>understand the mechanics</strong> behind commonly discussed trading concepts — not just memorize setups.</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Developing traders</strong> who have basic chart-reading experience and want deeper structural understanding.</li>
    <li><strong>Experienced traders</strong> looking for a clean reference that connects overlapping frameworks (SMC, ICT, classical price action).</li>
    <li><strong>Self-directed learners</strong> who prefer reading over watching hours of video content.</li>
  </ul>
</ConceptCard>

This is **not** a beginner tutorial on what a candlestick is or how to open a brokerage account. Some baseline familiarity with charting is assumed.

The content is organized into numbered sections that build on each other:

## Tone & Philosophy

Every section aims for the same standard:

- **Neutral.** Concepts are presented on their merits. No hype, no guru worship, no tribal loyalty to a single methodology.
- **Precise.** Definitions are explicit. When a term means different things in different communities, that's noted.
- **Practical.** Theory connects to observable chart behavior. Annotated diagrams show what the concept looks like in practice.
- **Honest about limitations.** No concept works 100% of the time. Failure modes and common traps are discussed alongside the ideal case.
