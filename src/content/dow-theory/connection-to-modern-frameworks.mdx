## Why This Matters

If you understand that a "Liquidity Sweep" is just Dow's "Phase 3 Excess" happening on a smaller timeframe, you stop viewing the market as a rigged casino and start viewing it as an organic psychological engine.

If you understand that an "Order Block" is simply the last downward "Minor Trend" before institutional "Accumulation" drove a breakout (BOS) in the "Primary Trend", you gain the confidence to trade without relying on magical indicators.

You are now ready to dive into modern Market Structure. Remember the tide, the waves, and the ripples.

<KeyTakeaways>
- Modern frameworks (SMC, ICT, Price Action) are primarily rebranding exercises of Dow Theory mechanics.
- A Change of Character (CHoCH) is simply a Dow Theory failure to print a new peak/trough.
- Break of Structure (BOS) is the Dow Theory confirmation of a trend continuation.
- SMT Divergence is the exact same concept as Dow's Averages Non-Confirmation.
</KeyTakeaways>
